This blog is to show my learning plan for my time at Dev Academy

What is your long term goal or career pathway?

To create solutions for our society and science based research on wildlife and their habitats.

A description of your strengths and limitations when it comes to learning

  • Strengths: I love working in a team environment, this helps in my learning, as learning together with others really plants concepts into my brain. I am hardworking, and even when I don’t understand what’s going on, I will push myself to get to where I want. It may take a bit longer with some things, but I get there which is the most important. Having done a lot of online learning over the past years, I have become extremely self-driven. Overall I love learning and developing new skills.
  • Limitations: If a lot of theory is thrown at me, I can get overwhelmed, and self-isolate. Need to work on taking breaks to get outside, exercise and breathe fresh air. And not eat unhealthy food if I feel stressed.

What skills (non-technical core/human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

I would like to work on my speaking confidence, not being scared to put my ideas out there.

A commitment to how you will manage your workload in this programme.

I am going to ask for help after 15-20 minutes of not understanding something. I need to make sure that I get outside, go exercise, and take lots of active breaks. As I have found, that's the only thing that can relax my brain. And taking time for myself, alone time is essential.

A commitment as to how and where you will seek help in a timely way

Talk to my team members, cohort, and facilitators if I feel I need help.

A description of what you expect from the Facilitation team

To demonstrate concepts in a visual way, not just theoretical. As I find it difficult with too much theory. I need hands-on projects to get stuff into my head.

Any scheduling information such as block-out times when you are committed to other things. For example, you might have whānau commitments like school drop-offs. It's important to plan these in.

Some weekends I need to make sure I hang out with my friends. Evenings or morning (whatever schedule I choose when I come to Wellington) for fitness.
